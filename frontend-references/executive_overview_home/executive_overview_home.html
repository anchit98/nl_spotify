subject Executive Summary

Users consistently express frustration with the new algorithmic discovery features introduced in v4.1. While overall engagement time has increased marginally, qualitative sentiment has dropped significantly regarding "control over playback".

The primary pain point centers on the inability to easily dismiss AI-suggested tracks. Conversely, the redesign of the library interface has been received positively, particularly among power users originating from Reddit cohorts.
